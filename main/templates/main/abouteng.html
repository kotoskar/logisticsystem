About us

The system capabilities:

  • The main idea of a product is a determine the location of drones in real time. Drones can used anywhere and works everywhere with help of this system.
  • The Web-site is used for display all necessary information, user can access from all date remotely with help of the base station.
  • Our system can foolow for 8+ drones and transmit theirs information such as a latitude and a longitude with the good accuracym and also the ressure.

About a system:

  • A System determine exact a location with help GPS and Glonass, the accuracy is near 4-6 metres. The Data is transmitted by the technology LPWAN with help of LoRa`s modules.
  • The scheme of receiver includes in Raspberry Pi 3 model B+ 4GB, GPS ATGM336H, BMP280, LoRa SX1278.
  • The scheme of transmitter includes in Raspberry Pi Pico, GPS ATGM336H, BMP280, LoRa SX1278.
